Orange Chicken

Ingredients

Sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of orange juice
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onion
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced fresh ginder root
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
 Chicken
  • 2 large skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 3 tablesppons olive oil

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients.

Step 2

Combine water, rice vinegar, lemon juice, soy sauce, and orange juice for sauce into a saucepan over
medium-high heat. Stir in brown sugar, green onion, orange zest, ginger, garlic, and pepper flakes;
bring to a boil. Remove from heat, and cool for 10 to 15 minutes.

Step 3

Place chicken in a resealable plastic bag. Pour in 1 cup cooled sauce. Seal the bag, and refrigerate
for at least 2 hours. Reserve the remaining sauce.

Step 4

Mix flour, salt, and pepper in another resealable plastic bag.

Step 5

Remove chicken from the marinade and place into the bag of seasoned flour. Seal the bag and shake to coat.

Step 6

Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook chicken in the hot skillet until browned on both sides.

Step 7

Remove to a paper towel-lined plate and cover with aluminum foil. Wipe out the skillet.

Step 8

Pour reserved sauce into the skillet and bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Mix together cornstarch and water
until completely smooth. Stir into the boiling sauce.

Step 9

Reduce heat to medium-low, add the chicken, and simmer, stirring occasionally, until cooked through, about 5 minutes.

Step 10

Serve over rice and enjoy!
